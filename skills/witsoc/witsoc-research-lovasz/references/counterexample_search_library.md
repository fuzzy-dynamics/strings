# Counterexample Search Library

Use this to pick standard counterexample families before ad hoc search.

## Families

### Graphs

- complete graphs,
- complete multipartite graphs,
- paths/cycles,
- random graphs,
- bipartite incidence graphs,
- critical graphs,
- blow-ups,
- sparse regular graphs.

### Number Theory

- prime powers,
- products of small primes,
- smooth numbers,
- squarefree numbers,
- CRT-constructed residues,
- valuation edge cases,
- local prime obstructions.

### Additive Combinatorics

- arithmetic progressions,
- random subsets,
- subgroups/cosets,
- Bohr-like sets in small cyclic groups,
- finite field examples.

### Geometry

- grids,
- points on circles/lines,
- degenerate collinear sets,
- finite field geometries,
- lattice configurations.

### Algebra/Logic

- small groups/rings/modules,
- quotient structures,
- nonfaithful representations,
- finite models with boundary cardinalities.
